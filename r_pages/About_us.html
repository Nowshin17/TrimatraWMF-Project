About Us

Trimatra Foundation established in 2016 under joint stock company aiming to establish medical waste management system in different city corporation. The Chairman of the company started her activity in the name of Private Waste Collection Service Provider (PWCSP) in Dhaka City Corporation (DCC) in 2000 & got her foundation registration in 2008. At that time the main activity was to develop house-to-house waste collection through van service. Now it formed a foundation to serve more and give more to the society and named Trimatra Waste Management Foundation.

Since then, Trimatra started its socio-economic, health-safety development activities for its manpower involved in waste collection & transportation. During implementing the waste collection activities we observed that a portion of health care waste got mixed with the general waste, which create dangerous problem for not only the waste collector but also the manpower of Dhaka North City Corporation, who are involved in the entire waste management system. To minimizing the existing problem Trimatra started its activity to collect general waste from the health care establishment in Dhaka North City Corporation (DNCC) in 2015. From then we are trying to establish eco-friendly Bio Medical Waste (BMW) management system for DNCC. In this regard we made a series of meeting with different donor agency, DNCC authority, DGHS and DOE. And at this moment we also got some definitive assurance for active participation in such initiatives.

Our Founder

Nahid Akter Lucky
Founder and President of Trimatra Waste Management Foundation

The founder of the Trimatra Waste Management Foundation is none other then Nahid Akter Lucky. She was actively invovle in various segments of waste managment for last 26 years and successfully and competantly completed her job for over last two decades. As for her experience she was the secretary of the Primary Waste Collection Service Provider (PWCSP) under Dhaka City Corporation from 2005 to 2008. And then became the President of Primary Waste Collection Service Provider (PWCSP) from 2009 to till now. She also worked as a Plant Incharge of Waste Concern from the year of 2000 to 2014, beside her works in PWCSP. Where she worked on mostly house hold waste and solid waste. Then she formed the Trimatra Ltd. in 2016 and as a Chairman of this company she directly work with Dhaka North City Corporation (DNCC) for Non-Hazard Medical Waste collection agency and in Joypurhat and Nowgaon City Corporation through the hospitals her venture with all Hazardous Medical Waste collection and management started.
